A satirical GPT clone for [De Speld](https://speld.nl) (essentially the dutch
version of The Onion). The idea came from my girlfriend, who works for De Speld. This was
published on their website and even gained a bit of attention on national
television.

Don't worry: no actual LLMs were used for this project.

My friends and I wanted to play Jenga with a chess clock, based on a [Tom Scott video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YN_F9bNuF0I), but we couldn't find a chess clock that supported more than 2 players. So that's what this project is, implemented in the simplest way possible as a static website. It works well on tablets too! I highly recommend trying chess clock Jenga.

Please excuse the terrible name.

title = "AUC Roomfinder"
description = "Helping you find your friends on the AUC campus."

The dorms of AUC have a terribly confusing layout and finding a room based on just the room number was very hard. So, my friends and I came up with an application to fix that. It just shows you on which floor and which section of the building the room you searched for is. 

Years after leaving AUC, I was interviewed by the university paper about the project, since it was somehow still actively being used.
